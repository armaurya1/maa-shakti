FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

What are your visiting hours?

Our general visiting hours are from 10:00 AM to 12:00 PM and 5:00 PM to 8:00 PM. ICU visiting hours are limited to 11:00 AM - 11:30 AM and 5:00 PM - 5:30 PM. Please contact the specific department for any exceptions.

Do you accept health insurance?

Yes, we accept most major health insurance plans including government schemes like Ayushman Bharat. We also have a dedicated insurance desk to help with cashless treatment and claims processing.

How can I book an appointment?

You can book an appointment through our website using the online booking form, call our helpline at +91 98765 43210, or visit our reception desk in person. We also offer appointment booking through WhatsApp.

What facilities are available for patients' attendants?

We provide comfortable waiting areas, a canteen with healthy food options, prayer room, ATM facility, and free Wi-Fi. For long-term patients, attendant beds are available in private rooms.

Do you provide ambulance services?

Yes, we have a fleet of fully-equipped ambulances available 24/7 for emergency services. Our ambulances are staffed with trained paramedics and equipped with life-saving equipment. Call our emergency number for immediate assistance.
